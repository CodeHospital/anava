Key Features

Discover what makes Anava the perfect companion for your spiritual journey across all belief systems

Spiritual Recording

Record your prayers, supplications, devotions, universal intentions, or karmic reflections with high-quality audio capture for personal growth.

Visual Waveforms

See your recordings visualized with beautiful waveform displays, helping you focus on rhythm and cadence in recitation.

Customizable Reminders

Set notifications for daily prayers, Salat times, Shabbat, meditation sessions, universal alignment practices, or any other regular spiritual practice that resonates with your beliefs.

Private & Secure

Your sacred recordings and spiritual reflections are stored securely on your device and never shared without your permission, respecting the intimate nature of your personal practice.

Spiritual Journey

Review your past recordings and track your spiritual journey over time, across holidays, holy days, celestial events, and significant personal observances.

Belief-Adaptable

Tailor the app to your specific spiritual tradition, practices, and needs, whether you're Jewish, Christian, Muslim, connecting with the universe, or following a karmic path.
